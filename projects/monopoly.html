Automated Monopoly Board

Electromechanical Monopoly board enabling human vs. bot gameplay — 1st place finish.

Overview

Led the electro-mechanical design of a robotic Monopoly board game, collaborating with a team of six to develop a fully automated system enabling human vs. bot gameplay.

Approach

Designed mechanisms, selected motors and PCBs, and integrated game logic with electromechanical systems.

Results

System reliably handles dice, piece movement, and card draw autonomously; showcased in competition.
