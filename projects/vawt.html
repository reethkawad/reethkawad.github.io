Vertical Axis Wind Turbine

Adaptive pitch control for a VAWT to enhance energy capture in low-wind urban environments.

Overview

Engineering an adaptive pitch control system for a vertical axis wind turbine to enhance energy efficiency in low-wind environments. Focus on optimizing blade design and mechanical power conversion through iterative simulation and physical testing.

Approach

Simulation-driven design, prototype fabrication, and control systems testing.

Results

Validated improved energy capture in urban wind profiles (experimental data and simulation).
