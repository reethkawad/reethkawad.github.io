Blowdown Wind Tunnel

Designed and fabricated a 1.5 m blowdown wind tunnel validating honeycomb flow straighteners.

Overview

Designed and built a 1.5-meter-long blowdown wind tunnel with a 100×100 mm² test section; integrated a 3D-printed PLA honeycomb flow straightener.

Approach

Constructed using plywood and acrylic, instrumented for velocity profile analysis.

Results

Demonstrated flow stabilization and repeatable test conditions up to Re ≈ 2000.
