Smart Alarm Clock — REM Monitoring

Arduino DAQ alarm that tracks BPM to wake users during optimal REM sleep — 1st place hackathon.

Overview

Built a smart alarm clock integrating a pulse oximeter sensor and Arduino-based data acquisition to track heart rate overnight and detect REM windows.

Approach

Used Arduino for DAQ, implemented signal processing to detect BPM trends, and prototype alarm logic.

Results

Prototype achieved reliable detection of BPM-based REM indicators and improved wakefulness experimentally.
