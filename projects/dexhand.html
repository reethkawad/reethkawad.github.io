8 DoF Robotic Hand

Dexterous robotic hand for USC's DRCL bipedal robot — tendon-driven with impact-tolerant MCP joints.

Overview

Designing and building a robotic hand for the Dynamic and Robotic Controls Lab (DRCL) at USC.

Approach

Tendon-driven actuation, passive DIP joints, spring-damper MCP mechanism.

Results

Prototype CAD and early test fixtures validated joint travel and impact tolerance.
