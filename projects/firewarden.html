Wildfire Defense Water Pump

Pool-fed automated spray system with MATLAB-modeled hydraulics for Palisades wildfire defense.

Overview

Developed a water pump system for wildfire protection by integrating a pool-based spray network with manual activation. Built MATLAB hydraulic models for pump sizing and pipe losses.

Approach

Modeled hydraulics in MATLAB; prototyped pump and manifold assemblies.

Results

Provided design documentation and MATLAB tools for sizing and deployment.
