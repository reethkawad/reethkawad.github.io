TIPS FOR STRONG STAR ENTRIES

SITUATION: One or two sentences max. What was broken, missing, or needed? What was the business/technical context?

TASK: Be specific about YOUR role. Not 'the team needed X' — 'I was responsible for X.'

ACTION: Concrete verbs + specific tools. 'Designed X in SolidWorks' not 'worked on design.' Include what was technically hard.

RESULT: Lead with numbers where possible (%, ms, tolerance, prize $, people supported). If no numbers, describe the downstream impact.

Quick result prompts: How much faster / lighter / more accurate? What decision did your work enable? Who used it? What broke before that didn't break after?

Skills row tip: List the 6–10 most searchable/specific tools and techniques. These map directly to recruiter keyword searches and ATS systems.
